Welcome to Chelmsford Sports Club

Our clubhouse provides the perfect venue for a wide variety of events, from corporate meetings and training sessions to private celebrations and community gatherings.

With modern facilities and a traditional club atmosphere, we offer a unique setting that combines professionalism with the warm hospitality that our club is known for.

Facilities & Amenities

  • Main function room (capacity up to 80 people)
  • Smaller meeting room (capacity up to 20 people)
  • Full catering kitchen facilities
  • Bar service available
  • Audio/visual equipment
  • Free parking for all guests
  • Accessible facilities
  • Beautiful views overlooking our sports grounds

Booking Information

Available for: Corporate events, private parties, meetings, training sessions, celebrations, and community events.

Capacity: From intimate gatherings of 10 people to larger events for up to 80 guests.

Catering: In-house catering available or bring your own caterer with our approval.
